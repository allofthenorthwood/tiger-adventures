 RE: Danny Bence's report  

Good work! Most people, Danny Bence included, assume that tigers, like domestic cats, are reluctant to swim across water. In fact, tigers love water and will readily spend an afternoon soaking in a cool pond or river. If a tiger really was interested in a tasty morsel such as Danny Bence (which I doubt), a few feet of water would not have stopped it.

Let's ask the police to search the area for pugmarks, but I think we already know the answer. I hope Danny's parents look kindly on this example of his active imagination.
