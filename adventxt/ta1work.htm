The Tiger Information CenterKey for Tiger on the Loose! Worksheet
on the Tiger Information Center's web site (www.5tigers.org)

1. Who are you? What's your job?

 

2. Where has the tiger escaped from?

 

3. Julia Allende's report about the missing tiger includes a plaster cast of a pugmark (pawprint). Do you think this pugmark was made by the runaway tiger? Why or why not?

 

4. Clem Taylor's report included some fur from a wild animal. Do you think this fur was left by the runaway tiger? Why or why not?

 

5. Jason Cooper may have spotted the runaway tiger in a tree. Do you think his report is reliable evidence of the tiger's whereabouts? Why or why not?

 

6. Danny Bence may have spotted the runaway tiger by the river. Do you think his report is reliable evidence of the tiger's whereabouts? Why or why not?

 

7. Victor Hernandez may have encountered the tiger at his ranch. Do you think his report is reliable evidence of the tiger's whereabouts? Why or why not?

 

8. Where do you think Police Chief Slayton should concentrate his search for the runaway tiger? Why?

 

9. After you learn the tiger's size and weight from Chief Slayton, what subspecies do you think the tiger is most likely to be? Why?
