"Clean skins?" repeated Ranjit. "Of course they are clean skins. Gopal does not use such crude methods as bullets or knives."

I thought about talking directly to Gopal in Hindi, but decided I would wait before revealing that I spoke their language. "Then how does he kill the tigers?" I asked Ranjit.

Ranjit shrugged. He cared only about the money, not about the methods. "Usually poison. Sometimes steel traps."

"How do you poison a tiger?" I asked. "It seems pretty hard to get close to one."

Ranjit looked at me like I was a stupid tourist. A flicker of concern flashed across Sanjoy's face, but then he smiled. Maybe he wanted the poachers to think I was a tourist.

"He doesn't poison the tigers directly," Ranjit explained. "He places poison inside dead buffaloes and cows, or in forest pools. The tiger comes to eat or drink and takes the poison also."

I shuddered at the thought. Still, I had to learn more. I said...

..."How many tigers has Gopal killed in the past year?"

..."It sounds dangerous. Why does he do it?"
