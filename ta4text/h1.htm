Mr. Singh picked me up several hours later. "Success!" he told me. "I wish you had been there. It would have made quite a story."

I sighed. He was right. Why had I come to India, if not to report from the front lines of the battle for the tiger's survival? "Do you have any more operations planned?" I asked him. "Maybe I could join you on the next one."

He nodded. "Certainly. We expect to close in on another poacher next week. I can't promise we'll have the same luck as tonight. But no matter what happened, it would be an enlightening experience."

I said...

"Sounds good to me."

"On second thought, I don't think I'm cut out for this. I'm going home."
