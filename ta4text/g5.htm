"Namaste," I said politely.

Mr. Singh looked up from his work and sighed. "You again," he replied in English.

"I've been studying," I explained. "I think you'll be impressed."

"Is that so?" he replied. A smile played on his lips. "Tell me, then--how much can a trader earn from the sale of the bones from a tiger?"
