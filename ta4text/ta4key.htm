The Tiger Information Center

Worksheet for Tracking the Tiger Trade Adventure
on the Tiger Information Center's web site (www.5tigers.org)

1. Who are you? What's your job? You're a journalist who spent ten years in India.

2. Why do you go to India? Because Dr. Tiger (Dr. Laohu) asks you to investigate the tiger trade and write an article that will raise public awareness about it.

3. Who do you meet in India? Mr. Sanjoy Singh, a Forest Service officer. He quizzes you on some basic tiger knowledge to make sure you know something about tigers, then invites you to join him on an undercover mission to arrest two tiger poachers.

4. How do the poachers kill tigers? With steel traps, or by poisoning dead animals or pools of water, thus poisoning any tigers that come to eat or drink.

5. Why do the poachers kill tigers? As one poacher says: "Tigers are not the romantic creatures that you foreigners think they are. They attack and kill our cattle, our goats, even our children sometimes. Gopal must protect his family from these predators. And he must earn money to support his family."

5. Where do you go after capturing the poachers? To Tibet, to follow the trade in tiger bones.

6. What does the Tibetan merchant offer you in exchange for tiger bones? Shahtoosh, the king of wool, made from the Tibetan Antelope, the Chiru, which is an endangered species. Shawls made from shahtoosh sell for more than two thousand dollars in Europe.

7. Where do you go to find the final destination for the tiger bones? Beijing, China.

8. What are tiger bones used for? Since ancient times, Chinese medical texts have recommended tiger bone as a cure for joint pain and stiffness, back pain, rheumatism, and muscle spasms. Ancient Chinese texts also recommended various other tiger body parts as the cure for many illnesses and health concerns. According to these traditions, tiger eyes improve your vision. Tiger whiskers cure toothaches. Tiger penises make tigers of men. These beliefs are deeply embedded in Chinese culture and cannot be changed quickly or easily.
