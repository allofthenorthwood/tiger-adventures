Ranjit and Gopal stared at me. "Arrested?" Then they laughed. "That seems very unlikely."

"You seem pretty confident." 

Ranjit pulled a gun from his pocket. It looked cheap and homemade, but very effective. "Anyone who tries such a thing will have to answer to this."

"I see your point," I said calmly. "Maybe you don't have anything to worry about. But tell me," I added, is that what you used to kill the tigers?"

"...It seems pretty messy. I want clean skins."
