Welcome to the official Web Page for Don Wilson's Wildlife Wonderland! Since 1978, this family owned and operated wildlife park has delighted visitors to the Rio Grande valley. You will encounter lions and tigers and bears (oh my!), as well as alligators, monkeys and many other animals.

News Bulletin: Save the Tiger!

The San Gabriel Zoo refuses to breed its male tiger with our beloved Toni the Tiger at Don Wilson's Wildlife Wonderland.
