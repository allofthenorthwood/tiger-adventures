The Tiger Information Center

Worksheet for Save the Tiger! Adventure on the
Tiger Information Center's web site (www.5tigers.org)

1. Who are you? What's your job?

 

2. Why does Don Wilson say the Zoo's tiger should be bred with his female?

 

 

3. What is your response?

 

 

4. What do you say to Don Wilson when he pickets your office?

 

 

4. What genetic criteria should you consider when selecting a mate for Jambi?

 

 

5. Which female tiger do you decide to mate with Jambi? Why?

 

 

5. What happens when Jambi tries to mate with the female tiger you chose?

 

 

6. At the end of the game, did you get a successful breeding? Are cubs born?
