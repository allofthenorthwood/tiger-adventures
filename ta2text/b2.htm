RE:Environment plan for tiger exhibit

I have developed a plan for the environment and enrichment elements of the new tiger exhibit. Please let me know if you have any questions. I look forward to working with you.

Environment/Enrichment Elements:

  • One scratching log
  • One raised resting platform
  • Two boomer balls
  • One scent trail using catnip to lead tiger to viewing areas
  • Plantings: Hawthorne (Crataegus sp.), locust (Robina sp.), morning glory (Ipomoea sp.)

Outlined below are two plans for the tiger exhibit's environment. Please contact us if you have questions about either one. We hope to hear from you soon.

Plan A Elements:

  • Two scratching logs
  • One raised platform for resting
  • One simulated creek with running water
  • One heat pad located near viewing areas
  • Plantings: Barberry (Berberis sp.), Sumac (Rhus sp.)

 

Plan B Elements:

  • One pool, six feet by twelve feet, two feet deep
  • One scratching log
  • One raised platform for resting
  • Two heat pads located near viewing areas
  • Plantings: Cedar (Cedrus sp.), juniper (Juniperus sp.), barberry (Berberis sp.)
