FROM: Anne Zimmerman
Director, San Gabriel Zoo
San Gabriel, New Mexico
RE: Location for new exhibit

I mentioned your choice of exhibit site to the Board of Directors. Several of them pointed out that it may not be the best location for the new tiger exhibit. As you know, our Zoo tries to arrange its animal collection in a zoogeographic pattern, or in groups of similar kinds of species. Your choice places the tiger among exhibits of Australian wildlife, which doesn't really make sense, since tigers don't live in Australia. Why don't you look into some of the other available sites?
