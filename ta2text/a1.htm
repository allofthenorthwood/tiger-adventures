TO: Zookeeper
San Gabriel Zoo
San Gabriel, New Mexico
FROM: Anne Zimmerman
Director, San Gabriel Zoo
San Gabriel, New Mexico
RE: New exhibit for tiger

Congratulations on your success in tracking and identifying the runaway tiger! I have received a number of telephone calls from the Zoo's Board of Directors and several City Council members and they would like to know if we can find a way to build an exhibit here at the Zoo. We have also gotten a lot of calls from the general public suggesting much the same. Please put some ideas together about how we could design and build an exhibit to house the tiger.

I talked to Dr. Laohu ("Dr. Tiger") yesterday. He is leaving for China tomorrow and will not be able to guide you through the design process. So plan carefully--you may not see the consequences of a poor decision until the exhibit is completed and open for the public. He did, however, send an update to his Tiger Handbook to answer your questions about exhibit design.

Keep in touch.
