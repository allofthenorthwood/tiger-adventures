RE:Tiger exhibit

Congratulations on a job well done! I've heard nothing but praise for the new tiger exhibit. Those hot wires were an inspired final touch. You avoided many pitfalls in the design process and should be proud of your accomplishment. Dr. Laohu has even asked if he might include the exhibit as an example of a good exhibit in his Tiger Handbook.

In the past few weeks I have been asked by both the Zoo's Board of Directors and several City Council members about the possibility of breeding this tiger. We have also gotten a lot of calls from the general public suggesting much the same. Why don't we look at that next?

Take a virtual reality tour of a tiger exhibit much like yours -- go to the large (570K) or small (210K) version (requires QuickTime VR plug-in).

Now that you've designed a good home for your tiger, play Tiger Adventure 3: Save the Tiger!, in which you must choose a good mate for your tiger. Or read scientific articles about designing a tiger exhibit and feeding program.
