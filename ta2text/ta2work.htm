The Tiger Information Center

Worksheet for Zoo Tiger Adventure
on the Tiger Information Center's web site (www.5tigers.org)

1. Who are you? What's your job?

 

2. Why does Don Wilson want the tiger?

 

3. Why does Barry Grady disagree with Don Wilson?

 

4. What important information does the veterinarian's report tell you?

 

5. Which area of the zoo would be the best place for the new tiger exhibit? Why?

 

6. Which exhibit design is the best? Why?

 

7. Which plan for the exhibit's environment is best? Why?

 

8. Which veterinarian has the best plan for caring for the tiger? Why?

 

9. What problems does your final tiger exhibit have? What mistakes did you make?
