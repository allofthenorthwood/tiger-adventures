Explore the Tiger Exhibit at the Minnesota Zoo

Take a look around the tiger exhibit from the inside!

  • Large panorama (for high-speed connections)
  • Small panorama (for slow connections)

To look left or right: click and hold the mouse button and move it to the edge of the picture
To zoom in: press the Shift (Windows) or Option (Macintosh) key
To zoom out: press the Control key

To view the panoramas, you must either have the Apple QuickTime Plug-in or a QuickTime VR player. Both can be downloaded at Apple's QuickTime VR Website. .
