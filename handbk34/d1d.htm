Zoo Tigers in the Wild?

If zoos could breed lots of tigers, we could let them loose in the wild, right? It is harder than that!

Tigers born in zoos always have always had humans to care for them. They wouldn't know how to live in the wild. They would have to learn to hunt, fight and breed in order to survive.

Tiger biologists are trying to think of ways to raise tigers that will have the skills to survive in the wild. What do you think?
