Peacocks and Other Birds

Tigers rarely prey upon peacocks or other birds. It takes too much energy to catch a bird that is such a small meal. Tigers may attack a peafowl if it seems easy, but nearby animals such as deer or monkeys usually raise an alarm. Unlike leopards or other cats, tigers are too heavy to climb trees to hunt, so birds can easily perch out of a tiger's reach.
