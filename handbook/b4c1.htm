Tigers which live in colder climates naturally need thicker coats to stay warm. Look how much thicker a coat the Siberian tiger has compared to the Sumatran tiger. Refer to the table for the lengths of fur for tiger subspecies.

Tiger Fur

Indochinese 7 mm to 35 mm (.25 to 1.35 inches)
South China 7 mm to 35 mm (.25 to 1.35 inches)
Siberian 40 to 105 mm (1.5 to 4.1 inches)
Sumatran 7 mm to 35 mm (.25 to 1.35 inches)
