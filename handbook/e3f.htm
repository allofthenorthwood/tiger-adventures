Tiger Parts: Brain

Ancient Chinese medical texts recommended tiger brains as a treatment to cure laziness and pimples. Although modern Chinese medicine does not recommend tiger brains, many Chinese still believe in their medicinal value. These beliefs are deeply embedded in Chinese culture and cannot be changed quickly or easily.
