Nallamalai Forest
The Nallamalai Forest is located in the southeast India, in the state of Andhra Pradesh. Tigers live in several parts of the forest, where the trees and grasses create a good habitat for them. However, a new state highway is being built through one prime tiger habitat, where about 30 or 40 tigers live.
