Udaipur

Welcome to Udaipur! As a tiger, you hunt:
deer, wild boar, and other game animals such as langurs, chevrotains, and hares.

You need to eat about 150 kilograms of game meat every month to survive. That's a lot of meat, especially since you usually eat only 60% of your prey.

So how is the hunting in Udaipur? Try hunting for several months and see whether or not you survive. Hunt!
