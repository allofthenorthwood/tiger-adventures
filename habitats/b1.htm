Udaipur

Udaipur is located in the western desert state of Rajasthan. A beautiful lake makes Udaipur a cool oasis in the Indian desert. Udaipur is also famous for an elegant palace on an island in the lake.

Udaipur has a population of 250,000 people and one tiger, which lives in the city zoo.
